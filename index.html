Think2Switch™

Many children living with disability may not have controlled movement of their body, impeding their ability to play and communicate. This can limit or even deprive them of opportunities to interact with the world around them and to participate with others. The Think2Switch™ is a revolutionary device that connects a commercial brain-computer interface to any switch-adapted device - toys, games, support devices - thereby allowing individuals to use the device just by “thinking” about it. The device is designed to be used at home and only takes about 30 minutes to set up. A brief 10-minute calibration prior to each session sets your loved one up to connect with their world in ways that might not have been possible before.
